About Me
I am presently employed at Universiti Malaysia Perlis (UniMAP) as a lecturer and researcher within the Faculty of Electronic Engineering & Technology (FKTEN). I successfully completed my doctoral studies in August 2023, having obtained my Master of Science in Computer Engineering from UniMAP in 2017. My academic journey commenced with a Bachelor's Degree in Mechatronic Engineering, which I earned in 2014. My research pursuits primarily revolve around Machine Learning, Internet of Things (IoT), and Wireless Sensor Network (WSN) technologies.
